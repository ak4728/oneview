Indicator Help

Simple guide for beginners
  • ATR Period: how many candles are used to measure volatility. Example: 10.
  • Multiplier: how strict trend bands are. Example: 3.0 gives fewer but stronger signals.
  • Confirm Bars: how long trend must stay before signaling. Example: 5 bars avoids noise.
  • BOS: break of structure. Trend continuation signal.
  • CHoCH: change of character. Possible trend reversal.
  • Order Block: zone where larger players likely entered before a move.
